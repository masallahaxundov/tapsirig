About Me

The cat (Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domestic cat.

Domestic cats are valued by humans for companionship and their ability to kill rodents. About 60 cat breeds are recognized by various cat registries.
